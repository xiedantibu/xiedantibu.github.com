Disch (12939)
IMO, CGI is a crutch that is overused. Not because the effect itself is bad or played out... but because it makes it really easy to make visually appealing movies that are otherwise empty.

Acting, writing, story, etc all become less important when you have good CGI. And good CGI is everywhere now... which is partially why modern movies really, really suck. Nobody is interested in creating substance anymore. As long as there's flashy explosions and cool effects, suckers will keep buying movie tickets.

See: any Michael Bay movie and any comic book movie... which comprise 95% of movies that have come out in the past 7 years.
