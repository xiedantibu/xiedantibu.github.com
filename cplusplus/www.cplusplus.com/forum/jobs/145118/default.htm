BAE Systems is looking for a Cloud Computing Integration Engineer in New Malden, UK

Cloud Computing Integration (Software Engineer)

BAE Systems is a global defence, aerospace and security company employing around 88,000 people worldwide. Our wide-ranging products and services cover air, land and naval forces, as well as advanced electronics, security, information technology, and support services.

We have a vacancy for a software engineer with integration experience in a virtualised environment to join the software products team, based in offices in New Malden, Surrey.

We are the sole supplier and integrator of combat management systems (CMS) for the Royal Navy’s surface and sub-surface fleet. We are developing a shared infrastructure (SI) virtualised environment that can host the CMS and other software to support planning, tactical picture compilation, decision-making and weapon control. Come join us and you could be developing the Systems that end up deployed in the Royal Navy’s fleet of Ships and Submarine, used to support the crew policing the world’s oceans, whether delivering humanitarian aid or in combat.

Your main responsibilities will involve:

Your role will be to develop, integrate and support the Shared Infrastructure environment, the CMS and its supporting toolsets.
Some of the benefits on the role and working for us include:

Experience of many aspects of the s/w lifecycle, such as: requirement definition, high & low level designs, integration, testing, product delivery, estimating and bidding.
Experience of working in a dynamic software team, working with numerous other teams across a number of projects and sites.
Wide range of career opportunities for both technical and managerial roles, with training available to support development.
Flexible working arrangements, to allow both the engineer and the business to adapt to each other’s needs.
A friendly workplace. Offices based on the high street of New Malden with numerous social and sports venues nearby give lots of opportunities to take part in activities at lunchtimes and outside work hours.
Job security. The nature of our work means we often work with long term projects. If you’re talented, we have work for you for many years to come.
Very experienced team members who will help develop your skills in systems, software and hardware disciplines

Your skills and experience will ideally include:

We require a Software Engineer with experience using integrating software in a cloud environment. Ideally, you’ll also have experience with some of the following:
VMware, Real Time Systems, Windows Server Administration, Citrix, Hyper-V, Linux Administration, Networking Cisco/3COM/Dell, Scripting Languages such as VBScript/PowerShell/PHP, Programming Languages such as Visual Basic/C/C++/C#

If you are invited for interview we will explore your knowledge of the skills we require that are included in your CV.

This is a full time position. We offer competitive salary, flexible working, competitive holiday allowance, pension, car scheme, share incentive plan, work life integration policy and child care vouchers.

BAE Systems aim to recruit the very best talent and welcomes applications from a diverse range of people, from all backgrounds and with many different skills, experience and perspectives.
